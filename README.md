# My-Pocket - Your personal consultant for income and expenses

My-Pocket uses your uploaded or manually entered transactions to determine your monthly budget, recurring payments, and savings goals. Automatic categorization and anomaly detection help you stay on top of your finances.

- When is my next rent payment due?
- Is this transaction normal? Why is it so high?
- Could I go out to eat this weekend based on my budget?
- How much did I save this month compared to last month?
- What subscriptions am I paying for and when are they due?
